From
tomorrow
on
,
I
will
be
a
happy
man
;

 

Grooming
,
chopping
,
and
traveling
all
over
the
world
.

  

From
tomorrow
on
,
I
will
care
foodstuff
and
vegetable
,

   

Living
in
a
house
towards
the
sea
,
with
spring
blossoms
.

    

From
tomorrow
on
,
write
to
each
of
my
dear
ones
,

     

Telling
them
of
my
happiness
,

      

What
the
lightening
of
happiness
has
told
me
,

       

I
will
spread
it
to
each
of
them
.

      

Give
a
warm
name
for
every
river
and
every
mountain
,

     

Strangers
,
I
will
also
wish
you
happy
,

    

May
you
have
a
brilliant
future
!

   

May
your
lovers
eventually
become
spouse
!

  

May
you
enjoy
happiness
in
this
earthly
world
!

 

I
only
wish
to
face
the
sea
,
with
spring
flowers
blossoming
.
